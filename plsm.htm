Course information

Plumbing sheet metal is a practical and theory oriented program.

There are three programs offered at one time per year however depending of the the course schedules. Technical

Training certificate (TTC) full time (2) years (40 weeks)

The minimum entry requirement is Grade 10 and 12 with B passes in English, Maths, Science and C grades in other subjects.

 

Career Outcome

Student graduating from Plumbing sheet metal are either absorbed into the Plumbing and sheet metal industry, companies, further studies or self employed.

 

 

Plumbing sheet metal Specialized studies

Plumbing sheet metal Department comprises of five (5) specialized trades within the industry,

  • Plumbing sheet metal
  • Metal
  • Plumbing tools
