Course information

Learn all the trade secrets of painters and decorators in this comprehensive course for apprentices. You will gain skills in all the practical and theoretical aspects of the trade and learn hands-on skill from our specialist teachers who are experienced industry professionals.

This is a nationally recognized trade qualification that covers works in residential and commercial projects. You will also learn all the technical and management skills which you will utilize both at work place and when you own and run painting and decorating business.

Full time two (2) years Technical Training certificate (TTC) course, 20 weeks National certificate 1,2,3 (NC) courses, Trade testing Level 1,2,3 are conducted at the college.

The minimum requirement entry is Grade 10 and 12 with B passes in English, Maths, Science, and C grades in other subjects.

 

Career Outcome

Painters and decorators are usually employed by painting, decorating and construction firms as an apprentice or are self-employed contractors.

 

 

Sign writing and painting Specialized studies

Painting and sign writing Department comprises of five (5) specialized trades within the industry,

  • Drawing
  • Sketching
  • Painting
  • Painting Tools and accessories
  • Paints

Painting and sign writing  services

The department not only train and skill students, they are also into service providence and income generation for the college.

The products they produce and sell include;

  • Notice or Bulleting boards
  • Banners
  • Sign posts
  • Cloths sign writing or drawings
