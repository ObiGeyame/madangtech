Cabinet Making and Joinery workshop and basic Computing class

Course information

Cabinet Making and Joinery is a practical and theory oriented program.

There are three programs offered at one time per year however depending of the the course schedules. Technical

Training certificate (TTC) full time (2) years (40 weeks)

The minimum entry requirement is Grade 10 and 12 with B passes in English, Maths, Science and C grades in other subjects.

 

Career Outcome

Student graduating from Cabinet Making & Joinery are either absorbed into the Furniture industry, companies, further studies or self employed.

CMJ Specialized studies

Cabinet Making and Joinery Department comprises of five (5) specialized trades within the industry,

  • Cabinet Making
  • Wood Machining
  • Upholstery
  • Wood Turning
  • Drafting/Drawing

Cabinet Making and Joinery services

The department not only train and skill students, they are also into service providence and income generation for the college.

The products they produce and sell include;

  • Tables
  • Chairs
  • Bunkers
  • Beds
  • Toolboxes
  • Coffin boxes
  • Shelves
  • Cupboards
