Diploma in Tourism and Hospitality

Diploma in Tourism and Hospitality Management courses help students acquire knowledge in tourism, leisure. hospitality concepts and learn how to apply them to a real-life tourism and hospitality business.

The course is professional course that deals with hotel management, resort management, customer service, accounts management and Travel/Tour management.

You will have two (2) years full time diploma course and 20 weeks National Certificate (NC)1,2,3 courses in Commercial cookery, Tourism Travel and Tour Guiding.

The Minimum requirement

  • Grade 10 or 12 with B passes in English, Maths, Science and C grades in other subjects.

 

Career Outcome

Diploma in Tourism and Hospitality Management can lead to exciting career opportunities in the tourism and hospitality industry.
