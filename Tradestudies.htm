Building construction

he department consist of two (2) sections. Building construction and sheet metal trade skills. The trade are combine together to provide relevant skills and knowledge to make up a complete integrated programme to prepare trainees in Carpentry, joinery and Block laying.

Full two (2) years Technical Training certificate (TTC) course, 20 weeks National Certificate 1,2,3 are conducted at the college

 

Painting and sign writing

Learn all the trade secrets of painters and decorators in this comprehensive course for apprentices. You will gain skills in all the practical and theoretical aspects of the trade and learn hands-on skill from our specialist teachers who are experienced industry professionals.

Graduates find employment in diverse fields like construction, business and tourism. The institution equips student with the necessary skills and knowledge for immediate employment or further education.
