Business Studies

A brief decryptions of all business and tourism studies offered at Madang Technical College.

This Diploma of Business Studies is designed to provide you with foundation skill in business and management.

The course equip you with knowledge and skills required in business accounting, economics, legal issues in business, management and marketing.

Excluding Tourism and Hospitality studies, students of Business studies are stream into Accounting, Management and Office administration at the second stage of the course.

The Tourism & Hospitality studies and all Business studies have the following three 3 levels of study in common;

  • The National Certificate (NC) level.

  • The Certificate level and

  • The Diploma level

 

Tourism and Hospitality course

Tourism and Hospitality course is a practical and theory oriented program.

There are three programs offered at one time per year however depending on the the course schedules.

Tourism and Hospitality studies is a full time (2) years (40 weeks) course.

 

Accounting course

Accounting course is a practical and theory oriented program.

In-order to be eligible for this course, one must first complete the business studies.

Accounting course is a full time (2) years (40 weeks) course.

 

Office administration course

Office administration course is a practical and theory oriented program.

Business studies is a foundation studies that a student should undertake before applying for this course.

The office administration course is a full time (2) years (40 weeks) course.

 

Management course

Management course is a practical and theory oriented program.

It is recommended that a student have a foundation year in business studies before undertaking this course.

The management course is a full time (2) years (40 weeks) course.
