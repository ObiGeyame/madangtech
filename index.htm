Our Mission

Institutional Profile

 

History

It was established since 1957 which was 18 years before the independence year of Papua New Guinea. It runs under the care and directions from the national education department until 2021.

Established since 1957 as a junior technical school for basic technical studies, enrolling students from grade 6 to form 1 to 2.

 In 1972 it was elevated to the status of a technical college, expanding enrolment to form 3 and 4 students and offering training in secretarial work, welding, motor mechanics, bricklaying, plumbing, carpentry, and boat building.

During 1996, it faced challenges during the rationalization program, shifting focus to become a Building and Allied Trade College and losing the boat building course.

Universal Primary Education policy: Implementation led to a decline in resources and student enrolment, prompting strategic reforms to restore stability and growth.

Courses and affiliation

The Institution is a Papua New Guinea government's nationally recognized  college and is affiliated  with the department of higher Education Research Science and Technology (DERST).

Last 2022, the Department of Higher Education Research Science and Technology (DHERST) took over from Education department after the reform made by the NEC decision.

Under the new reform of education system the college extend to offer accredited courses in business and tourism studies and these courses will provide pathway for students further education and training with other institutions.

Students are awarded with certificates after completion of various trade studies, certificates and diplomas for business studies after two (2) years completion of studies in both Business and Trade courses.

Financial sustainability

About 90% of its income comes from course fees and 10% from other income sources.

Location and services

Madang Technical college is located right in the heart of Madang Town. The campus is safe and secured 24 hours with security service provided by the security guards. It has a 30 sitter bus for pick-up and drop-off service for students and staff.

The college has girls and boys dormitories and mess facilities. A standby Generator to supply electricity for the entire college.

The Principal is the head of this institution and he's assisted by the two deputy principals including support from the college registrar and a board of governing council. It has energetic and dedicated full-time and part-time working staff with certified trade & business qualifications and professional experiences.
