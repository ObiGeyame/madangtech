Diploma in business studies

The Diploma of Business Studies is designed to provide you with foundation skill in business and management.

This course equips you with knowledge and skills required in office administration, accounting, economics, legal issues in business, management and marketing.

You will have two (2) years diploma course and 20 weeks National Certificate (NC) courses in business studies.

The entry requirement

  • Grade 10 or 12 with B passes in English, Maths, Science and C grades in other subject.

 

Career Outcome

Completion of the Diploma of Business Studies will prepare you to pursue employment opportunities in roles such as Office administrator, project officer, administrator personal assistant, accounts personals.
