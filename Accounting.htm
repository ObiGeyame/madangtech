Accounting studies

Diploma in Accounting courses help students acquire knowledge in business concept, business documents, transaction analysis and financial reports and learn how to apply them to a real-life accounting in business.

The course is professional course that deals accounting for petty cash, Journalizing, transaction analysis, Trial balance and balance sheet.

You will have two (2) years full time diploma course and 20 weeks National Certificate.

 

Entry requirement

  • Students must pass the first stage - foundation in business to qualify for this study.
